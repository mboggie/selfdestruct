Selfdestruct is a simple service that monitors your Twitter stream, and for tweets you mark with #sd, schedules them to be automatically deleted.

Usage:

  • #sd on a tweet = that tweet disappears in five minutes.
  • Customize the time-to-live of any tweet by adding a number of minutes after the #sd tag (so, #sd20 will live for 20 minutes)
