selfdestruct

selfdestruct is a simple service that lets you control the life span of your tweets. Any tweet you mark with #sd is scheduled to be automatically deleted.

  1. Sign in with your Twitter account below to activate the selfdestruct service.
  2. Add #sd to any tweet and that tweet will disappear in five minutes.
  3. Customize the life span of any tweet by adding a number of minutes after the #sd tag (so, #sd20 will live for 20 minutes)
